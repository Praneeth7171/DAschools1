Fire Safety Drill!

Ready to be a Fire Fighter!

We need to practice what to do when we smell smoke or hear the smoke alarm. Our goal is to get out fast, stay low, and go to our **Safe Meeting Spot!**

Step 1: ALARM! Get Out and Stay Out!

If you hear the smoke alarm, **get out immediately!** Do not stop for toys or pets. Once you are outside, **NEVER go back inside** for any reason.

Step 2: CRAWL Low to the Ground!

Smoke rises! If you see smoke, **crawling is faster and safer.** Get on your hands and knees. Check doors for heat with the **back of your hand** before opening them.

Step 3: STOP, DROP, and ROLL!

If your clothes catch fire, remember this superhero move: **STOP** where you are, **DROP** to the ground, and **ROLL** over and over to put out the flames!

Step 4: Go to Your Safe Meeting Spot!

Every family needs a safe place outside, like a neighbor's tree or mailbox. Go there right away! Once everyone is safe, an adult should call **911** or their local emergency number.

Fantastic! You Beat the Fire!

You followed all the steps and made it to safety! Practicing these steps makes you a fire safety expert and helps keep your whole family safe.
