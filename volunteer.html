Apply to volunteer for organizing and executing school disaster preparedness drills.

Guidelines & Safety Rules

  • Commitment: Volunteers must commit to a minimum of 5 hours per month.
  • Training: Mandatory 3-hour orientation and safety training must be completed before participation.
  • Confidentiality: All student and school data must be kept strictly confidential.
  • Chain of Command: Volunteers must follow the instructions of the designated Drill Coordinator at all times.
  • Safety First: Personal safety equipment (vest, whistle) must be worn during all drill simulations.
  • Professionalism: Maintain a professional and calm demeanor to ensure a realistic and safe environment for students.
