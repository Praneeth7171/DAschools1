Evacuation Guide: Know Your Steps

  • 1. Assess and Alert: Check for immediate danger (fire, smoke, etc.). Immediately activate the fire alarm or call emergency services if the situation requires it.
  • 2. Grab Your Go-Bag: Take your pre-packed emergency kit (Go-Bag) which should contain essentials like medication, water, first aid, and critical documents.
  • 3. Use Designated Routes: Follow the routes shown on the map above and posted signs. **Do NOT** use elevators during a fire.
  • 4. Check Doors: Before opening a door, check if it's hot. If it is, use an alternate route. Close doors behind you to slow the spread of fire/smoke.
  • 5. Go to Assembly Point: Proceed directly to the **designated external assembly point**. Do not stop to retrieve belongings or return to the building.
  • 6. Check-In: Report immediately to the person in charge at the assembly point so that officials can confirm everyone is accounted for. **Do NOT re-enter** the area until cleared by emergency personnel.

For non-emergency route inquiries, contact your local facilities or safety manager.
