Earthquake Safety Drill!

Ready for an Adventure?

Uh oh! What if the ground starts shaking? Don't worry, we'll learn how to be super safe! Let's practice **"Drop, Cover, and Hold On!"**

Step 1: DROP like a Pancake!

As soon as you feel wobbly, quickly **drop to your hands and knees!** This helps you stay steady and ready to move.

Step 2: COVER your Head!

Crawl under a strong **table or desk**. If you can't find one, huddle next to an inside wall and **cover your head and neck with your arms like a helmet!**

Step 3: HOLD ON Tight!

**Hold onto a table or desk leg** so it stays with you. Keep holding on until the shaking completely stops. You're doing great!

Hooray! You're an Earthquake Hero!

Fantastic job! You've learned how to stay safe. Remember to always be prepared and practice with your family and friends!
